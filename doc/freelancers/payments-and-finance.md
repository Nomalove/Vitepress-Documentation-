# Payments & Finance for Freelancers
 
Wrapped automates your financial workflow.
 
### Key Features
- Split view between **labour** and **equipment** income.
- Cash payments can be confirmed manually.
- Production companies mark payments as made; freelancers confirm receipt.
- Invoices and reconciliations are automatically recorded.
- Coming soon: **Fintech integration** for direct payments.
