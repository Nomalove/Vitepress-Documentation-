# Freelancers on Wrapped
 
Freelancers are at the heart of Wrapped.  
You can create your own professional profile, link with agents, showcase your experience, and get booked faster.
 
Every freelancer profile includes:
- Display name, roles, and experience level
- Location(s) and travel availability
- Portfolio and professional references
- Daily rate (with negotiable or private options)
- Health and dietary preferences (optional)
